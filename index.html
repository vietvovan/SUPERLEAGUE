The Super League mission
Our Mission
Several months ago, the formation of the Super League came at the time that global COVID-19 pandemic had accelerated the instability in the existing European football. Unfortunately, 9 out of 12 clubs had pulled out of the proposed tournament that they signed up early. That means, the tournament will be likely cancelled. Moreover, COVID-19 pandemic has been affecting every aspect of the global community, especially health and finance.
In this situation, Super League is formalized with the mission to support the global football community and contribute to the world charity funds through our charitable partnerships.
Our mission is to develop an ecosystem dedicated to sport enthusiasts:
  • SLE token is created mainly for football communities
  • Supporting the global community is our toppriority
  • All token holders in SLE ecosystem have full rights to own and drive the platform fairly and transparently
  • SLE token is building real utilities that will create and enhance value

How to Buy
How to buy SLE on Pancakeswap?
1. Download Metamask
For Desktop: Turn on your Google Chrome then visit metamask.io . Download the MetaMask extension and set up a wallet. On mobile: Get MetaMask’s app for IOS or Android.
2. Setup Binance Smart Chain on Metamask
Select Settings from the dropdown menu.
On the Setting page, locate to the Networks menu.
Click Add Network in the top-right corner to manually add the Binance Smart Chain. Add information as below:
Network Name: Smart Chain
New RPC URL: https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org/
ChainID: 56
Symbol: BNB
Block Explorer URL: https://bscscan.com
3. Transfer BNB to your Metamask wallet
Transfer BNB to your new MetaMask wallet from your existing BNB BEP-20 wallet addresses start with 0x, you can see the address on metamask or your BEP-20 crypto wallet by clicking on Buy or Receive on Metamask/Crypto Wallet.
4. Swap BNB for SLE
Go to Pancakeswap.
https://exchange.pancakeswap.finance/#/swap?outputCurrency=0xb3f0b61252b71f7a731ba27b2adef14a07795d84
Remember to set slippage tolerance to 2-4% before swaping.
Now you can start swapping BNB to SLE.
5. View your SLE in Metamask
To view your SLE balance, add the custom token

FAQs
How to buy SLE on Pancakeswap?
1. Why Super League?
Not only football communities but also the world has been affecting by COVID-19 for almost 2 years since it first happened. In view of this, Super League provides a comprehensive and quality platform for football fans to support the world community while making profits by joining/playing Super League games during this COVID-19 pandemic.
2. Is the contract audited?
The smart contract is under auditing and we will release the information as soon as possible.
3. What is static farming?
2% of the transaction value from selling or transfering transactions on the Super League ecosystem will be allocated proportionally to all SLE holders. As a result, overall circulation will progressively reduce, the Market Cap will increase as the SLE holder's balance will also rise.
4. What are the main products of Super League?
Fan Token Prediction, Club Price, Simulated Super League along with Community Protection.
5. What is the mission of the Super League?
Building an ecosystem dedicated to sport enthusiasts.
6. Why Binance Smart Chain?
Binance Smart Chain (BSC) is the best choice as one of the largest DeFi community with low transaction fee and high performance.
7. What is the purpose of fundraising?
In addition to raising funds to develop the project, Super League Finance will establish a fund to assist the global community that are experiencing financial hardship as a result of the COVID pandemic. This fund is called Super League Charity Fund.
The Super League Charity Fund will be distributed to global community funds in the world, to which the destinations will be decided by Super League members via Governance mechanism.
Straight 10% of total supply will be sent to this fund, while it will be increased by the cash flow of SLE games in a fixed-rate, to which also will be decided by Super League members via Governance mechanism.
